Research

Our team strives to create a more efficient and effective navigation system for the Canadian Space Agency website. The redesign hopes to increase traffic to the site and provide users with a better understanding of the CSA's mission. The UX/UI team conducted interviews, created a new style design, and prototyped a new CSA website.  The UX/UI team tested the original site, created a thoughtful redesign that also included a new aesthetic, and prototyped a responsive web redesign.

Problem

The Canadian Space Agency site was designed for Canadians and others interested in Canada’s impact on space science. The main purpose of the site is to support the development of space science, bring awareness to careers and internships available, and discover and stay up to date on the latest news in space science and research. The mission of our site is to allow users visiting to successfully navigate and find helpful information to support and grow their knowledge of the Canadian Space Agency

Final Design

The CSA redesign team was able to design a better functioning site that serves to create an easier user flow increasing overall user experience. The team was also able to design a more aesthetic design serving the CSA missions and purpose. The team identified problems such as the confusing navigation and overabundance of information and created a sleek and more thoughtful design.
